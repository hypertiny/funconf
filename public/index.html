Funconf is a conference on a bus, in Ireland.

Whilst embarking on a bus-tour of the Irish countryside, funconf attendees will listen to stories from some of the brightest folks of the online world. The emphasis is on getting up close and personal with the speakers. The atmosphere is intimate, informal: a moving bus, literally.

The venue is the bus. 35 limousine-style seats. Attendees will sit facing each other along the edge, while speakers present their stories from the head of the bus.

The trip will start with breakfast in Dublin, before attendees embark on their story-telling tour. Along the way, the ‘conference’ will stop in Kilkenny for a traditional Irish lunch and in Waterford for the day’s ‘keynote’, before heading back to Dublin for a dinner and wrap up event in Dublin’s Guinness Gravity bar.

Speakers on the day will include Michael Lopp (aka Rands), author of Managing Humans and blogger at Rands in Repose, Marshall Kirkpatrick, lead writer on ReadWriteWeb.com, Jonathan Siegel, serial entrepreneur and founder of ELC Technologies and Joe Stump, former Lead Developer of Digg, co-founder of SimpleGeo.

Funconf is presented by Dublin technologists Paul Campbell and Eamon Leonard, in an effort to bring a unique Irish perspective to the world of technology conferences.
